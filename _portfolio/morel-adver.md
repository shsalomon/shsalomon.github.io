We propose [MOREL](https://github.com/salomonhotegni/MOREL), a method to improve deep neural network robustness against adversarial attacks using a multi-objective optimization approach. 
Our training method uses an embedding space in which cosine similarity loss and multi-positive contrastive loss are applied to align natural and adversarial features from the model encoder and ensure tight clustering.
